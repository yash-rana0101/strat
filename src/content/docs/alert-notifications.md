## Real-Time Alerts

Never miss a major shift in market dynamics. Set up instant event notifications:

### Supported Destinations

- **Telegram & Discord Webhooks**: Receive alert summaries in your communication channels.
- **Desktop/Mobile System Tray**: Direct system notifications through our native desktop application.
