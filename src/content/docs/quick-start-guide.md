## Welcome to Strat Ai

Strat Ai is a professional quantitative trading terminal for Indian equity derivatives (NSE F&O) markets. This guide gets you up and running in under five minutes.

### 1. Register for Access
Enter your email on the waitlist or log into your account using the credentials provided during early access onboarding.

### 2. Configure Your Terminal Layout
Select your preferred grid view (Single-chart, Dual-chart, or Multi-monitor Option Chain layout).

### 3. Track Your First Stock
Search for a ticker (e.g., `RELIANCE` or `NIFTY`) in the top search bar to view its real-time trend overlay and consensus conviction score.
