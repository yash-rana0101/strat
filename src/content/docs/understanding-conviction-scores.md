## The Conviction Score

The conviction score represents the mathematical consensus of our five market dimensions (Technical Analysis, Volatility Matrix, Order Flow Metrics, Option Chain Analytics, and News Sentiment).

### Reading the Indicator
- **80% - 100% (High Bias)**: Strong directional consensus. Indicates that multiple indicators align on trend direction.
- **50% - 79% (Moderate Bias)**: Mild directional alignment. Often observed during initial breakout phases.
- **Under 50% (Neutral/Consolidating)**: Low conviction. Indicates market consolidation or conflicting indicators.
