## Navigation Guide

The Strat Ai interface consists of four primary quadrants:

1. **Watchlist & Ticker Finder (Left)**: Instantly track active NSE stocks, derivatives, and sectors.
2. **Main Chart Pane (Center)**: Displays price action overlaid with mathematical volatility projections and trend continuation bands.
3. **Indicator Grid (Right)**: Shows real-time readouts of RSI, VWAP, MACD, and Order Flow imbalances.
4. **Options Chain Ladder (Bottom)**: Displays strike-ordered Open Interest and volume profiles.
