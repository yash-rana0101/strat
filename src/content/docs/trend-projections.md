## Trend Projection Overlays

Rather than plotting lagging indicators, Strat Ai overlays mathematical trend projections directly onto the chart interface.

### How Boundaries are Calculated
Our models evaluate recent closing volatility, standard deviations, and volume weights. They plot dynamic bounds representing potential exhaustion limits where a trend is statistically likely to consolidate.
