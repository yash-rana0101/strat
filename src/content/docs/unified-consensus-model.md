## Deep Dive: Consensus Model

Strat Ai uses a multi-faceted evaluation pipeline to cross-check market signals:

### 1. Technical Framework
Tracks classic indicators like RSI crossovers, MACD histogram momentum, and VWAP deviations across multiple timeframes.

### 2. Volume & Open Interest Analysis
Analyzes option chain build-up, PCR trends, and shifts in max pain levels.

### 3. Market Sentiment
Parses headlines and news feeds to estimate short-term sentiment momentum.
