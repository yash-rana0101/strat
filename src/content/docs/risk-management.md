## Strategic Capital Preservation

Preserving capital is the number one rule of trading. Strat Ai incorporates risk guardrails:

### Position Sizing
Calculates target allocations based on volatility (ATR) and the consensus conviction score. High-conviction setups allow for standard sizing, while lower-scoring zones suggest reduction.

### Strategy Self-Defense
Alerts you to high-risk conditions like excessive IV percentile shifts or rapid contract roll-overs.
