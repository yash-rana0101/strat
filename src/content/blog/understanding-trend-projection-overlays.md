## Volatility and Trend Boundaries

Understanding trend boundaries is key to retail risk management. Mathematical regression models applied to recent closing prices assist you in mapping potential trend continuation and exhaustion zones.

## Actionable Breakout Zones

By overlaying these volatility bands, the terminal identifies areas of trend exhaustion, helping you avoid buying at the absolute high or selling at the absolute low.
