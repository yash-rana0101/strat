## The Danger of Single-Indicator Trading

Discretionary traders often fall victim to confirmation bias — searching for indicators that confirm their pre-existing market bias while ignoring warning signs. Fusing multiple technical inputs and open interest data points helps filter out false breakouts and provides a balanced consensus.

## Fusing Diverse Parameters

Our consensus engine evaluates options chain metrics, volatility skews, and indicator trends to generate a singular conviction score, reducing emotional bias and boosting risk management.
