## Real-Time Synchronization

Derivatives pricing moves fast. Relying on delayed option chain screens makes it nearly impossible to trace sudden shifts in institutional positioning.

## Tracking Intraday Shifts

With real-time options data, you can view the changes in open interest as they happen, helping you spot resistance walls and support floors before they trigger sudden price moves.
