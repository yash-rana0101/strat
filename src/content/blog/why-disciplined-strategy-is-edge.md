## Overcoming Emotional Bias

Emotional decisions are the leading cause of capital destruction in Indian options markets. Disciplined backtesting and robust risk rules form the foundation of professional trading.

## Capital Preservation First

Prioritizing capital protection ensures you stay in the game. With systematic strategy tools, you can plan entry targets and position sizing with high clarity.
