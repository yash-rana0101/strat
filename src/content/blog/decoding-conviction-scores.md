## Understanding the Evaluation Pipeline

A conviction score is the culmination of evaluating multiple technical dimensions. Instead of focusing on a single trendline, our consensus model checks multiple signals simultaneously to generate a unified percentage score.

## Eliminating Single Points of Failure

Relying on a single indicator often leads to false signals. By fusing multiple technical, options, and market sentiment variables, the terminal ensures you have a robust bias assessment.
