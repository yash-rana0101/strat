## Reading the Option Chain Volume

Open Interest (OI) buildup provides a map of where sellers are positioning themselves. Looking at PCR shifts and max pain changes helps traders identify where the major participants expect Nifty to settle on expiry day.

## Using Heatmaps for Visualization

By visualizing OI changes in a structured heatmap, it is easier to spot strike-ordered support/resistance zones and trace breakout momentum before it reflects on the underlying index.
